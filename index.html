Introduction

Welcome to Peekaboo Pals, a kids memory card matching game developed by Famz Games ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application available on Google Play.

We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children. Peekaboo Pals is designed to be a safe, fun experience with no personal data collection.

Simple summary: We do not collect, store, sell or share any personal information from players of Peekaboo Pals.
2

Information We Do NOT Collect

Peekaboo Pals does not collect any of the following:

  • Name, email address, or any account information
  • Phone number or device contacts
  • Location or GPS data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Payment or financial information
  • Social media profiles or login data
  • Voice recordings or biometric data
3

Information Collected Automatically

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements and Firebase (Crashlytics + Analytics) to monitor performance. These third-party services may collect limited, non-personal technical data including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and crash reports
  • General usage patterns (e.g. which difficulty level is selected)
  • Advertising ID (used for ad serving only)
Important: This data is collected by Google's SDKs under their own privacy policies. We do not have direct access to personally identifiable information from these services.

You can review Google's privacy practices at: policies.google.com/privacy

4

Advertising

Peekaboo Pals is free to play and is supported by ads served through Google AdMob. Ads may appear as banner ads at the bottom of the screen and occasional interstitial ads between game sessions.

  • We use Google AdMob's family policy settings for child-directed treatment
  • Ads shown are appropriate for all ages
  • We do not use personalised advertising targeted at children
  • AdMob may use the Advertising ID on your device to serve relevant ads

You can opt out of personalised ads through your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

5

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Peekaboo Pals is designed for children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • No registration or account creation is required
  • No chat, messaging, or social interaction features exist in the app
  • No links to external websites or third-party apps are accessible from within the game
  • All advertising is served in compliance with child-directed content policies

If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information about a child, please contact us immediately at famzgamesstudio@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

6

Data Storage & Security

Peekaboo Pals stores only your high score locally on your device using Android's built-in storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Is not accessible to any third parties

We take the security of our app seriously and regularly review our code to ensure it meets the highest standards of safety for young players.
